ABOUT STRIKE GHANA

Ghana's Premier Sports Products Brand - Where Performance Meets Identity

Our Story

Strike Ghana Ltd, a distinguished subsidiary of YAC Group Holdings, stands at the forefront of the sports apparel and equipment industry in Ghana. Headquartered in the vibrant heart of the country, we take pride in our commitment to excellence.

Our strategic partnerships with reputable manufacturing facilities in Europe and Asia enable us to merge international expertise with a deep understanding of local needs. This unique blend empowers us to create premium-quality products specifically designed for the diverse weather conditions across Africa.

Our range of offerings includes performance-driven apparel, cutting-edge sports equipment, and innovative accessories tailored to enhance the training and competitive experiences of clubs, athletes, and sports enthusiasts.

At Strike Ghana, we are dedicated to supporting every individual in their athletic journey, ensuring they have the right gear to achieve effective performance and reach their full potential in their chosen sports.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted sports apparel brand, celebrated for quality, innovation, and impact—transforming athletes' performance and uplifting the image of local sports clubs to international standards.

Our Mission

We exist to inspire and empower through sports image by providing high-performance, affordable, and durable apparel and equipment, bridging the gap between professionalism and branding for local sports clubs, collaborating with teams to build strong identities that resonate with fans and communities, and championing sustainable practices that support the long-term growth of Ghana's sporting ecosystem.

About the Ghana Premier League

The Ghana Premier League (GPL) is the top professional football division in Ghana. Established in 1956, it has grown to become one of Africa's most competitive leagues, featuring 18 clubs from across the country.

Strike Ghana is proud to be an official partner, providing high-quality kits and equipment to several GPL teams.
