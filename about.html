Our Story

Strike Ghana is a distinguished sports brand rooted in Ghana, with manufacturing partners spread across Europe and Asia. We specialize in designing and producing high-performance athletic wear tailored for athletes and fitness enthusiasts.

Our commitment extends beyond mere apparel; we meticulously craft our products to enhance comfort, style, and performance during various physical activities. A key focus of our brand is on elevating the professionalism and branding of local sports clubs, ensuring they stand out in a competitive landscape.

As a household name in Ghana, Strike Ghana is proud to have collaborated with some of the country's most esteemed teams, including Asante Kotoko, Accra Great Olympics, Liberty Professionals, Bechem United, Legon Cities FC, and Nsoatreman, amongst others.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to become synonymous with high-quality athletic apparel that not only meets the functional needs of athletes but also fosters a strong identity for local clubs. We aim to create a lasting and positive impact on the community by enhancing the professional image of these clubs, enabling them to thrive both on and off the field.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear: to bridge the existing gap between professionalism and branding for local sports clubs in Ghana. We endeavor to elevate the professional image of local sports clubs, making them more recognizable and respected, cultivate and promote a compelling brand identity for these clubs that resonates with their target audiences, and identify and address the discrepancies between the current and desired levels of professionalism and branding.

Our Core Values

Professionalism

We are dedicated to excellence in every aspect of our work, from product design to customer service.

Branding

We believe in the importance of creating unique and powerful brand identities that help clubs stand out in a crowded market.

Sustainability

Our aim is to develop sustainable practices that support both our brand and the local clubs we serve, ensuring they continue to thrive in the long run.

Quality & Durability

We prioritize high-quality materials and craftsmanship to ensure that our products withstand rigorous use while being affordable for our customers.
